What's new on ORBIS

Two new working papers by Walter Scheidel (May, 2013)

Explaining the maritime freight charges in Diocletian's Price Edict and The shape of the Roman world are available for download.

New web site sections: "Media" and "What's New" (May, 2013)

Orbis has received a lot of press attention, and we have begun a "refresh" of the site; look for more improvements soon.

An ORBIS API (January, 2013)

The ORBIS application programming interface (API) has been released, allowing programmatic access to some routes and site data. Thanks to the efforts of the Pelagios Project, by mapping many ORBIS sites to place name entries in the Pleiades digital gazetteer, ORBIS is now (modestly) a part of the Linked Open Data cloud.

ORBIS|via released (June, 2012)

ORBIS|via is an interactive application offering a situated perspective of travel across the Roman world, allowing you to travel the network in a fashion similar to the great early video game Oregon Trail.
